About Us

Building Character Through Knowledge Since 1974

For over five decades, Westbrook Academy has stood as a beacon of educational excellence. Our holistic approach combines rigorous academics with character development, preparing students not just for college, but for life.

We believe every student possesses unique talents waiting to be discovered. Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and vibrant community create an environment where curiosity thrives and leaders emerge.
